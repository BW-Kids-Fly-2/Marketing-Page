How It Works

#1 Sign Up

You will first sign up here and fill out our sign up for so we can know you and your needs. Signing up is a short proccess with you in mind. We understand the busy life of a parent and we want you to have a smooth experience from sign up to boarding your flight.

#2 Plan Trip

Once you have purchased your flight ticket, you can enter your trip details in our 'Plan Your Trip' form. Entering all the information of your flight such as the airport, airline, date, and time you will arrive will give your assistant the right information to ensure you have a smooth and easy experience.

#3 Choose an Assistant

Once all of your information is available, our well trained assistants will be able to view and apply to service you. You will recieve notification within 2 hours of the assistants who are available for you and you can view their profile and rates and make your decision on who is best for you!

#4 Relax On Your Trip

Once you have chosen your assistant, you are now on your way to a stress free check-in and onboarding of your flight. Your assistant will have arrived before your arrival time per your request and will be ready to take the weight of your baggage off of your shoulders so that you can check in and enjoy your family. Enjoy!
