About Us

We at Kids Fly 2 understand the importance of having a smooth flight experience from arriving at the airport to landing in your destination. We also understand that it is rare to have a smooth experience and even more so difficult when you have children with you. Kids Fly 2 was created in March 2020 with parents and kids in mind. Our goal is to allow parents the freedom and relaxation of being with their kids and having a smooth experience from the arrival at the airport to the arrival at the destination. Our assistance are carefully interviewed and trained in order to ensure parents and kids have the best flight experience.

Our Mission

Our mission is to offer our services at every airport world wide. We want everyone to have a better experience with their kids when flying. We also want to expand the job market allowing more jobs and less unemployments.
